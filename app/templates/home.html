About CampusHire

CampusHire is a placement management platform designed for universities to streamline the recruitment process. It enables companies to create placement drives, students to apply for opportunities, and administrators to manage and monitor the entire placement cycle.

For Students

Discover placement drives, apply to companies, and track your application status easily.

For Companies

Post placement drives, review student applications, and shortlist candidates efficiently.

For Admin

Manage companies, approve drives, and monitor placement statistics from a centralized dashboard.
